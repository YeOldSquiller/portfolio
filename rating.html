The importance of this is high because it helps create a good and well created game. Without specific things being asked you'll never know if something is wrong with your game or disliked.

    • Importance of Quick Ratings

    Quick ratings help determine if specific things about your game are good or not. From sprites to level design, if it's not good then your game wouldn't turn out as fun as you'd of thought. By averaging out each category to get a final result, you can tell which should be better or needs to be better.

    Importance of Short Answer Ratings

    Short answers can help you figure out if there is anything wrong with your game that you couldn't see. Let the person playing the game tell you what's wrong with it. You can figure out what people like best and do more of it, or give them a voice and give some option on what you could add to the game.
