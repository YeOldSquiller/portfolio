Background

Objective

Player’s controls
  • Space background.

  • Planet Destroyers, also known as humans are evading your planet.

  • Defend the moon before they get to your planet. If you die, the world dies.

  • Kill the Enemies.

  • Get the highscore.

  • Survive.

  • S is to go/rotate left.

  • D is to go/rotate right.

  • Space bar is to shoot.

  • Mouse is to aim where you want to shoot.

    Modifications

    Mod # Short Name Description Status/Functionality
    1 Spawner The spawner in the game is important because it makes the game infinite. Without it, the game would quickly end making it a boring game. It also makes it more interesting by not having a number count to the amount of enemies. Working
    2 Scoreboard The scoreboard gives the game a purpose. Without a scorekeeper you would just be killing enemies. Challenge your friends to see who gets further. Working
    3 Shooting This is important to the game because you wouldn’t be able to play the game without. The enemies would just kill you making it a complete different game. You would just run from the enemies which you would easily lose because you can only move in a circle. Working
