    Analysis and Reflection on Survey Results

    FishPod v2 was better than FishPod v1 according to the data I received. On all of the questions asked, the answers were increased. The graphics average increased by 1.5 from the last score. The Sprites average increased by 0.375 from the last score. Simplicity of controls average increased by 0.25. Audio average increased from 4.5 to 4.625, increasing by 1.25. Lastly, simplicity of objectives was increased by 0.60. I think the scores were better because of the modifications added. People seen things added that they like and wanted so it made a better perception on the general game. Truly the only things changed were one control, and objectives.
