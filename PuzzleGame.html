Objective

Player’s controls
  • W, A, S, D is to move up, left, down and right..

  • Find the ending towards a wall, leave the room and be sent to another room

  • The background is tiles sets as brick walls and dark dirt ground

  • Torches are random lights around the map

  • Beat the game by going through all the levels

    Features

    Feature # Short Name Description Status/Functionality
    1 Lighting The light makes the game more difficult. While showing little parts of the maze around you, you can’t see the full maze. The darkness is where the end could hide. Working
    2 Torch The torch shows a small circle of lighting than the player itself. It can always be seen randomly across each stage. This help players see different paths of where to go and where not to go. Working
    3 Shaders The shaders make the game look and run nice. This will make people want to play your game more. Our shaders were for the walls and the lighting of the darkness around. Mostly Working

    Analysis and Reflection on Survey Results

    I think my game was overall pretty good. According to my survey results which was mainly 4’s and 5’s, the game turned out to be really good. There was no one area that had all low scores, randomly there we’re 3’s and 1’s. But if I were to say that replayability had the lowest score and functionality had the highest score. There was a troll poller that did pick all 1’s. Something to make my game better would be to add better levels. Also to add more of a reason to play, like powerups, or a timer. The reason our games wasn’t perfect is because there isn’t much of a purpose to the playing part. Our player sprite needs to be changed and the background tile for the ground needed to be aligned correctly. Overall it’s little details for our games to make it successful and fun to play.
